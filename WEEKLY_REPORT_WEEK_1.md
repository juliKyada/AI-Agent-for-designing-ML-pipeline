## Tasks Completed (4 Marks)

Summarize the key tasks completed during the week.

- **Task 1: Literature Review** - Reviewed AutoML frameworks including Auto-WEKA, Auto-sklearn, TPOT, FLAML, and LightAutoML. Documented optimization strategies (Bayesian optimization, genetic programming, random search)
- **Task 2: Meta-Learning Analysis** - Analyzed meta-learning approaches and warm-starting techniques in Auto-sklearn and OpenML repositories
- **Task 3: Problem Formulation** - Documented the Combined Algorithm Selection and Hyperparameter optimization (CASH) problem and identified limitations of existing approaches
- **Task 4: Research Gap Identification** - Identified that most systems use metadata only for initialization but revert to black-box search, suggesting a need for rule-based constraint approaches
