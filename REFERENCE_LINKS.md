## 6. Metalearning for algorithm selection (Leite & Brazdil)

The paper uses a *survey* on metalearning/algorithm selection. Two common, real options:

- **Option A — Springer survey (recommended):**  
  - **Title:** Metalearning: a survey of trends and technologies  
  - **Link:** https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s10462-013-9406-y  
  - (Authors: Brazdil et al., 2014; similar content to “metalearning for algorithm selection” surveys.)

- **Option B — MLR 2021 (Leite & Brazdil):**  
  - **Title:** Exploiting Performance-based Similarity between Datasets in Metalearning  
  - **Link:** https://proceedings.mlr.press/v140/leite21a.html  

If you use Option A, update the citation key and year in `references.bib` to match.
